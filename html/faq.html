Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our services and processes

What is Growvate and what services do you offer?

Growvate is a digital growth agency specializing in social media management and virtual assistant services. We help small to medium businesses in India grow their online presence and streamline operations through strategic social media management, content creation, community engagement, and comprehensive virtual assistance support.

Where are you located and do you work with clients outside India?

We're based in Mumbai, India, but we work with clients globally. Our services are not limited by geography, and we can effectively serve businesses across different time zones and regions.

How long have you been in business?

Growvate was founded in 2023. While we're relatively new as a company, our team brings over 8 years of combined experience in digital marketing, social media management, and business operations.

What makes Growvate different from other agencies?

Our unique approach combines personalized strategy with affordable pricing, making professional digital services accessible to small and medium businesses. We focus on building long-term partnerships rather than just providing services, ensuring sustainable growth for our clients.

Which social media platforms do you manage?

We manage all major social media platforms including Instagram, Facebook, LinkedIn, Twitter, YouTube, and TikTok. The specific platforms we work with depend on your package and business needs.

How often do you post content?

Posting frequency varies by package: Starter (12 posts/month), Growth (20 posts/month), and Pro (30 posts/month). We create a content calendar based on your industry best practices and audience engagement patterns.

Do you create original content or use stock photos?

We create a mix of original content and high-quality stock photos. We can work with your existing brand assets, create custom graphics, and use premium stock photos to maintain professional quality while staying within budget.

How do you measure success and provide reports?

We track key metrics including engagement rate, reach, follower growth, click-through rates, and conversions. Reports are provided monthly (Starter), weekly (Growth), or daily (Pro) with actionable insights and recommendations.

What tasks can your virtual assistants handle?

Our virtual assistants can handle administrative tasks, email management, data entry, customer service, research, scheduling, document preparation, social media monitoring, and e-commerce support. We tailor the services to your specific business needs.

How do you ensure data security and confidentiality?

We take data security seriously. All team members sign confidentiality agreements, we use secure communication channels, and we implement strict access controls. We never share client information with third parties.

Can I communicate directly with my virtual assistant?

Yes, you'll have direct communication with your assigned virtual assistant through your preferred channels (email, WhatsApp, or project management tools). We also provide regular updates and check-ins to ensure everything runs smoothly.

What if I need more hours than my package includes?

We offer flexible scaling options. You can purchase additional hours at a discounted rate, or we can upgrade your package. We always try to accommodate urgent requests within reasonable limits.

What are your pricing packages?

We offer three main packages: Starter (₹15,000/month), Growth (₹25,000/month), and Pro (₹45,000/month). Each package includes both social media management and virtual assistant services. We also offer custom packages for specific needs.

Are there any hidden fees or setup costs?

No hidden fees! Our pricing is transparent. There's a one-time setup fee of ₹5,000 for new clients, which covers initial strategy development, account setup, and onboarding. All monthly fees are clearly stated in your package.

Do you offer discounts for long-term contracts?

Yes! We offer discounts for annual contracts: 10% off for 6-month contracts and 15% off for 12-month contracts. This helps you save money while committing to long-term growth.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, UPI payments, and online payment gateways. Invoices are sent monthly, and payment is due within 7 days. We can also set up automatic recurring payments for your convenience.

How long does it take to get started?

We typically begin work within 1-2 weeks of project approval. This includes initial consultation, strategy development, account setup, and content planning. For urgent projects, we can expedite the process.

What happens during the onboarding process?

Onboarding includes a detailed discovery call, brand analysis, competitor research, content strategy development, account access setup, and team introduction. We also establish communication protocols and reporting schedules.

Can I change my package or cancel services?

Yes, you can upgrade, downgrade, or modify your package at any time with 30 days' notice. We don't lock you into long-term contracts, and you can cancel services with the same notice period.

What if I'm not satisfied with the results?

We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee. If you're not happy with our services, we'll work to fix the issues or provide a refund. We're committed to your success and will do whatever it takes to meet your expectations.
